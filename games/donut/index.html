🍩 How to Play

🏪 Buy your donuts

Open the Shop before each wave to buy donut warriors. Each type has different strength, range and speed. You can have up to 20 on your team.

👆 Move your donuts

Tap a donut to select it (it glows), then tap anywhere on the arena to send it there. Drag a box across multiple donuts to select them all, then tap to move the group.

🛡️ Defend → ⚔️ Attack → ☠️ Boss

Waves alternate: first defend your base from attackers, then attack the enemy base. Every 10th wave is a Boss — much tougher. Survive all 30 waves to win!

💰 Sell & upgrade

In the Shop you can sell weaker donuts for 75% of their cost and reinvest in stronger ones. If you run out of donuts and coins it's game over!

💖 Lives

You have 3 lives. Lose a wave and the wave replays — lose all 3 and it's game over.
